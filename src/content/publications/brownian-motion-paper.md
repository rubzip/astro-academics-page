# Abstract

In this paper it will be shown that, according to the molecular-kinetic theory of heat, bodies of a microscopically visible size suspended in liquids must, as a result of thermal molecular motions, perform motions of such magnitude that they can be easily observed with a microscope...

It is possible that the motions to be discussed here are identical with so-called "Brownian molecular motion"; however, the data available to me on the latter are so imprecise that I could not form a definite opinion on the matter...
